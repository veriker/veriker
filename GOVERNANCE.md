# Governance

This document is an honest description of how Veriker is run today and where
we intend to take it. We would rather state the real situation plainly than
dress single-company stewardship up as a foundation.

## Today

Veriker is built and maintained by the team at Nexi Technologies, Inc.
Decisions about direction, releases, and what is in scope are currently made
by that team. There is no independent steering committee yet, and we are not
going to pretend there is one.

## Commitments

- **Open development.** Issues, pull requests, and design discussion happen in
  the open on the public repository.
- **Released code stays open.** See [NO-RELICENSE.md](NO-RELICENSE.md).
- **DCO, not a CLA.** Contributions are accepted under the Developer
  Certificate of Origin — we do not take copyright assignment. See
  [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Direction

We intend to broaden stewardship beyond a single company over time — bringing
in outside maintainers with real commit and review authority, and moving the
reference verifier and bundle schema toward a neutral home. We have a standing
internal commitment to donate the reference verifier and schema to a neutral
open-source foundation; we will publish the concrete terms here when they are
agreed rather than announce a date we have not yet earned.

If you want a larger role in Veriker, the path is ordinary and open:
contribute, review, and help maintain. That is how maintainership is earned
here.
